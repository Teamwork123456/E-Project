Our Educational Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a leader in innovative education

Our History

Milestones that shaped our institution's legacy

1995

The Foundation

Founded by three visionary educators in a small rented building with just 47 students and 5 faculty members. Our initial focus was on vocational computer training.

2002

First Accreditation

Received full accreditation as a degree-granting institution, marking our transition from a training center to a full-fledged college.

2008

Campus Expansion

Moved to our current 25-acre campus in DHA Phase 5, featuring state-of-the-art labs and the first digital library in the region.

2014

International Recognition

Our Computer Science program ranked among the top 100 in Asia by the Global Education Index.

2019

Online Learning Initiative

Launched our comprehensive online learning platform, making quality education accessible to students across Pakistan.

2023

Present Day

Now serving over 5,000 students annually with 200+ faculty members across 15 departments, while maintaining our commitment to personalized education.

Our Educational Philosophy

At COURSE College, we believe education should be transformative, accessible, and relevant to the real world. Our approach combines rigorous academics with practical skills development, preparing students not just for jobs, but for meaningful careers and lives of purpose.

We pioneered the "Learn-Apply-Innovate" methodology that has become our trademark, emphasizing hands-on learning through projects, internships, and community engagement.

Our Evolving Campus

From our modest single-building origins to our current 25-acre learning ecosystem, our campus reflects our growth while maintaining the intimate learning environment that defines the COURSE experience.

Today, our facilities include cutting-edge research labs, innovation hubs, collaborative learning spaces, and recreational facilities designed to nurture both intellectual and personal growth.
